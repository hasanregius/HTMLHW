Figure Subheading


Figure 1C: Batrachochytrium dendrobatidis prevalence and infection intensity results from Cameroon (n=1760; unpublished data from this study and Hirschfeld et al 2016 only). Blue line represents Bd infection prevalence with 95% binomial confidence intervals; green line is the average infection intensity (the logarithm of zoospore equivalents or ZScore) with standard error; green diamonds represent the maximum Zscore for each time period. Green hashed horizontal line indicates a Zscore value of 4, which is the threshold for deadly infection intensity (Vredenburg et al 2010).
